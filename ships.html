There are many ships available. In this section we will look at some of the early ones and what they are used for to make sure you buy a ship suited for your play style.

Sidewinder

The Sidewinder is your basic ship and one you will always have available to you even if you have no money. It works solidly at a low level for almost any career choice except trading. Although it's damage output is low, it does have a reasonable maneuverability making it not completely terrible in a fight. You will want to upgrade this relatively soon to take advantage of the abilities afford to you by more specialised ships.

Eagle

The eagle is the obvious fighter upgrade from the Sidewinder. It outperforms the Sidewinder in almost every aspect. It is faster, more maneuverable, better armed and has a better array of guns. The only drawbacks you might find in it's power management and in it's lack of armour. A good first upgrade and a solid recommendation here.

Hauler

The hauler is a relatively inexpensive trade ship that sacrifices any ability in combat for a modest cargo. It's armour is paper thin and it has hardly any weapons. In case of combat, run. Unless you are planning on trading commodities between star bases, this ship is probably not for you. It does have a half decent jump range and, with engineering, can top 40ly per jump. Even so, as an early pilot I would advise you stay away from this one.
